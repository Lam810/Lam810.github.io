Tsartung Zeteng LIN

Assistant Research Fellow

Visiting Scholar at SUSTech，Permanent Researcher at CESSR-SUSTech，Permanent  Member of World Econometric Society，PhD at Hong Kong University of Science and Technology.

中国青年经济学者联谊会（YES）第八届学员，中国科学院大学应用经济学暑期学校第三届学员，山东大学公共经济与公共政策研究生暑期学校学员，厦门大学王亚南经济研究院马克思主义政治经济学暑期学校学员，中国现场统计研究会高维数据统计分会第四届学员，嘉兴学院第一届香樟西部计划优秀学员。

研究方向为计量经济学，生物统计学和计算社会科学。已发表国内外期刊论文十余篇，其中中科院分区SCI一区期刊论文2篇，SSCI一区期刊论文1篇。

英文已发表作品如下所示：

[1]Chen Jie, Narisong Huhe, and Lin Zeteng. (2022), Economic Modernization vs. Political Contingency: Understanding Cohort Shifts and Democratic Support in China. [J]. Perspectives on Politics : 62.2 : 302-329.
[2]Lin Zeteng. (2022) SlMIR164A regulates fruit ripening and quality by controlling SlNAM2 and SlNAM3 in tomato. [J]. Plant Biotechnol. Journal 
[3]Lin Zeteng.  (2021), The pattern of DNA methylation alteration, and its association with the changes of gene expression and alternative splicing during phosphate starvation in tomato. [J]. The Plant Journal : for cell and molecular biology, 108: 841-858.
[4]Lin Zeteng.(2022) The Influence Mechanism Between Structural Monetary Policy & Financing Constraints of SMEs (April 27, 2022). [C]. Available at SSRN
